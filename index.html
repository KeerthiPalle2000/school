Sri.K.Bassappa as Chairperson and Smt.K.Vijayalakshmi as Founder. Since its inception of 18 years it has grown by leaps and bounds as a unique institutions with high ideals of imparting quality education even to a common man who cannot afford for expensive education as such helping the child in providing not only good education but also all round development of the child. The School have energetic, hardworking and dedicated staff who always extends their services with the motto of Work is Worship.

At present our School runs with 1500 students, 75 teachers under the leadership of our Correspondent Sri Koppunoori Rajeshwar, Executive Member Mrs. Koppunoori Veena, Head Mistress Mrs. M.V.Padmavathi, Vice-Principal Mr.D.Venga Babu. Now in this new era a child is working like a machine and leads life with over burden of studies but Vijaya High School is the one which helps the child to develop mentally and also physically by providing him all round development. Every child in our School feels as second home. As a part of all round development of the child, the School encourages in providing modern education with well equipped Computer Lab, Science Lab, Library Volleyball, Hockey, Karate, Drawing, Music and Spacious Playground.
